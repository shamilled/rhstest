About Us

A little bit about ourselves

Renaissance Healthcare Services is a Healthcare Agency, Licensed by the State of Virginia, Bonded and Insured and has been acknowledged as one of the most regarded Healthcare Agencies.

Renaissance Healthcare Services provides a wide range of trained and support services. We provide care to those with disabilities; special needs, recovering from surgery or illness. We run one of the most efficient and dependable Healthcare Services in the whole of Northern Virginia, and its surrounding areas.

We are committed and dependable in meeting the maximum standard of care by demanding quality of work from our well-seasoned caregivers. As such living up to our ultimate mission.

Our Mission

We uphold ourselves to the highest standards and moral code

Three words: Effectiveness, Efficiency, and Excellence, are words that best describes our mission to our valuable clients. Also to improve the quality of life of those we serve, and to provide professional, compassionate, reliable and timely care to each of out clients.

Our ultimate goal is to provide our clients with the best service that will satisfy them and also to give their families peace of mind. We recognize that we have been given the important responsibility of caring for the family members and loved ones of others; our values are that every one of our clients deserves to be treated with the respect and compassion that we would expect for our own family members.

To ensure this we employ professional caregivers with experience. They are also trained on the peculiar nature of each case before assigning them to clients.

Our caregivers have a genuine care and love towards work and that’s the only way they can work in our services. Your loved ones deserve to have all the comfort of home and that’s exactly where we’ll care for them.

Effectiveness | Efficiency | Excellence

Our highest priority is the selection of our caregivers. Our caregivers have a passion for caring, plus the skills and commitment to help our clients remain independent and at home. They are highly trained, understanding, skilled and experienced in caring for the elderly.

Our caregivers work closely with patients and families with care and compassion and we endeavor to create a supportive and caring relationship whether it’s short or long term service we are providing.

They are carefully and properly screened with thorough background checks including motor, vehicle record history, social security validation and diligent reference checks before being hired.

We provide Live-in/Live-out, Short-term/Long-term and hourly cases. We are available 24 hours a day 7 days a week. We work with Certified Nurse Aides (CNA’s) and Home Health Aides (HHA's).

Certified | Experienced | Compassionate
